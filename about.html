About Us

Mallamma Nudi is a digital newspaper platform that provides daily editions in an easy-to-read online format.

Our mission is to deliver accurate, timely, and relevant news to readers across regions. We focus on local news, community updates, and important events that matter to our audience.

With our e-paper system, users can browse daily editions, view pages, download PDFs, and clip important news articles easily.

We are committed to providing a smooth and user-friendly reading experience across all devices.
